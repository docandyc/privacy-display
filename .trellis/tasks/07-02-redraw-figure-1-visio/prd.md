# Redraw paper Figure 1 in Visio to IEEE Access standards

## Objective

Create an editable Visio reconstruction of `paper/figures/concept_threat.png` using the approved editorial two-panel design in `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-02-figure-1-visio-design.md`.

## Acceptance criteria

- The canvas preserves the 3:2 source aspect ratio at a 3.50-inch single-column target width.
- Panel (a) clearly depicts an authorized user, sensitive display, and one smartphone physical-capture threat.
- Panel (b) clearly depicts four rapid complementary subframes, human temporal integration, single-frame camera sampling, readable output, and OCR failure.
- All scientific labels and `PRIVATE DATA` displays are editable text.
- Human and camera outcomes remain distinguishable without relying only on color.
- Deliverables include `.vsdx`, `.svg`, `.pdf`, `.png`, and the source `scene.json`.
- Strict validation, audit, visual review, and no-op gates pass without blocking defects.
- Final PNG is 300 dpi; final PDF is one vector page.
- Existing `paper/main.tex` and `paper/figures/concept_threat.png` remain unchanged.
